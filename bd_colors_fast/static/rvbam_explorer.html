RV Content Diagnostics

FieldMeaning
data_contrastIntrinsic fractional structure in the observed segment after flux uncertainties are accounted for; higher values mean the data carry more RV-bearing features.
model_contrastTemplate 99-to-1 percentile fractional contrast; higher values mean the model has stronger line structure to anchor an RV.
nmodel_10p_contrastCount of model pixels at least 10 percent below the high-flux reference; low counts flag line-poor segments.
noutliers_maskedHigh-residual fit pixels masked before the final segment RV fit; large counts flag problematic wavelength regions.
