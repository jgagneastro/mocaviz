MOCAdb JavaScript data visualizations

This page lists the faster JavaScript versions of the MOCAdb visualization tools. Choose one of the following tools:

  • Brown Dwarf Photometry Explorer

    Compare MOCAdb objects against photometric, chemical equivalent width or spectral index sequences versus colors or spectral types with MOCAdb sequence overlays.

  • Brown Dwarf Evolution Explorer

    Compare MOCAdb brown dwarfs with adopted or BANYAN Sigma membership ages against Sonora Diamondback extended evolutionary tracks with selectable physical axes.

  • Gaia CMD Explorer

    Inspect Gaia color-magnitude diagrams for selected associations and individual MOCAdb objects.

  • MOCA Explorer

    Browse selected association members across CMD, XYZ/UVW, rotation, Gaia activity, H-alpha, and lithium panels with shared object selection.

  • BANYAN Sigma

    Run BANYAN Sigma from manual observables or MOCAdb-loaded values, with compact top-result and stored MOCAdb comparisons.

  • Group Hierarchy

    Browse the parent-child structure of MOCAdb associations and jump from selected branches into reports, member queries, and JS XYZ maps.

  • Spectral Typing

    Fit and compare near-infrared spectra against standards, with optional dereddening or slope corrections.

  • Spectral Explorer

    Load, normalize, and compare selected MOCAdb spectra in an interactive Plotly viewer.

  • Spectral Index Explorer

    Inspect one spectrum with the MOCAdb wavelength windows, continuum model, and calculated spectral-index or equivalent-width components.

  • SED Explorer

    Inspect object-level photometry, spectra, bandpass responses, scaled templates, and empirical bolometric SED integrations.

  • Atmospheric Retrieval Explorer

    Explore MOCAdb retrieval T/P profiles, cloud parameters, chemical abundances, contribution functions, and retrieval diagnostics.

  • Astrometric Explorer

    Visualize equatorial-coordinate measurements over time with proper-motion, parallax, reference, and recalibration controls.

  • Spatial-kinematic Explorer

    Explore XYZ positions and UVW velocities for association members, candidate objects, and model ellipsoids.

    Access a lighter Plotly version here: /js/xyz-plotly.

  • Dual XYZ/UVW Explorer

    Inspect fixed XYZ and UVW 3D views side by side with shared controls, native camera navigation, and the ESO Galaxy background in XYZ.

    Access a lighter Plotly version here: /js/xyz-dual-plotly.

  • Age PDF Explorer

    Review available age probability density functions including those from MOCAFlows for objects or associations, including summary table metadata.

  • MOCA Flows

    Inspect MOCAFlows observable age PDFs, association stacks, full forward-model products, and Fe/H-aware panel metadata.

  • RVBAM Explorer

    Browse RVBAM sampling runs, segment radial velocities, diagnostic figures, parameter summaries, and bounded posterior-sample views.

  • Moranta et al. 2026 Rotation Explorer

    Explore transferred Mora26 TESS rotation periods across lambda m values, classifiers, literature periods, and linked light curves.
